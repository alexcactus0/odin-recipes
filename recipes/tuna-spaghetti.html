Tuna-Spaghetti

Home

Description

This tuna spaghetti recipe is a simple pasta dish that's perfect for a quick dinner!

Ingredients

  • Pan
  • Spaghetti
  • Tuna can
  • Black pepper
  • Yellow Kurkuma powder

Steps

  1. Add water in the pan.
  2. Turn the heat up and wait until it starts boiling.
  3. Add the Spaghetti.
  4. Add a solid amount of salt.
  5. Let it cook for 10-15 minutes.
  6. After that pour the Spaghetti in a colander to drain the water.
    (This will free up the pan for preparing the Tuna.)
  7. Lower the heat then add in the Tuna including its Oil.
  8. Add a solid amount of black pepper and the yellow kurkuma powder
    and let it cook for 3-4 minutes.
  9. After that pour the Spaghetti on the cooking Tuna and mix it together.
    (Of course you can turn off the heat while you mix it.)

*optional:

  1. Add a great amount of any type of mayonnaise you prefer
  2. Add herb cream cheese (Recommended)

That's it Enjoy!
